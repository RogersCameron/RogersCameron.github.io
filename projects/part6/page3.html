Helldivers 2

Helldivers 2" is praised for its co-op shooter gameplay mixed with comedy, where teamwork, strategy, and the use of Stratagems play a critical role in overcoming challenges. The game emphasizes coordination and the clever use of resources in battles against various enemy types, offering a variety of missions and environments. Its humor, alongside the strategic depth and co-op design, makes it a standout experience, especially for those seeking a fun, cooperative game​

StarWars Outlaws

Star Wars Outlaws" is set between "The Empire Strikes Back" and "Return of the Jedi," offering an open-world adventure as Kay Vess. Developed by Ubisoft Massive, the game features vast environments, seamless land-to-space travel, and a dynamic Wanted system. A faction reputation system influences the world based on your choices, shaping missions and interactions.

Banishers: Ghost Of New Eden

Banishers: Ghosts of New Eden presents a deeply emotional and narrative-driven action adventure, set in a world where players take on the roles of Red and Antea, a duo of ghost hunters navigating the challenges of love, loss, and the ethical dilemmas of dealing with the supernatural.The game intricately combines detective work and combat, where players unravel haunting mysteries and make choices that significantly impact the story and world around them.

Tekken 8

Tekken 8" is celebrated for its evolution, offering an array of single-player content, excellent training tools, and a great online experience. The game introduces new mechanics that heighten the excitement of matches, including the Heat System for dynamic battle shifts.The roster includes 32 diverse characters, both returning and new, each with unique moves and customization options.

Skull and Bone

Skull and Bones" is praised for its immersive naval combat and extensive ship customization, creating a compelling experience for fans of high-seas adventures. However, it primarily focuses on naval warfare, lacking character-focused action like sword fighting. The game features a variety of activities, including crafting, which is a significant part of gameplay. While it may not fulfill all aspects of the pirate fantasy, it offers an engaging experience for those interested in naval battles​
